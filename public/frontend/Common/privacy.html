Privacy Policy

Updated: 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how CaneMap collects, uses, and protects your information.

1) Information We Collect

  • Account data: name, email, role (e.g., farmer, worker, SRA officer)
  • Field data: field name, location, size, documents you upload
  • Activity data: task logs, actions taken in the system

2) How We Use Your Information

  • Identify users and manage access to features
  • Show fields and task logs in maps and dashboards
  • Notify you of approvals, remarks, and important updates

3) Who Can Access Your Data

  • You (your own profile and activities)
  • Authorized roles (e.g., field owners, SRA officers) when needed
  • System administrators for support and maintenance

4) How We Protect Your Data

  • Authentication via Firebase (secure login)
  • Role-based access to data
  • Secure storage of documents in the database and cloud storage

5) Your Rights

  • Request correction of incorrect information
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to approvals and records)
  • Ask questions about how we handle your data

6) Contact

For privacy concerns, contact your SRA representative or the system admin.
